Animal Science




What is Animal Science?

Animal Science is the studying of the biology of animals that are under the control of humankind. It is also known as the production and care of farm animals.


What does Animal Science do?

Animal scientists work with farm animals, wildlife, laboratory animals, pets, and zoo animals. They study these animals to help keep them healthy and productive. Animal scientists help a lot to put food on our tables, and they improve animal breeding, growth, and nutrition.
